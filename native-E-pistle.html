No 1 About Project

The E-Pistle Super App is a transformative digital platform developed in partnership with my home church, AIC Ngeleltarit, to ease spiritual engagement and church operations. Inspired by the church's commitment to fostering a connected and spiritually enriched community, achieved through a blend of modern technology and faith-based values.

Key Features:

  • Centralized Digital Repository:Access sermons, Bible verses, hymns, and event archives in one place, ensuring consistency and accessibility for all members.
  • Event Management: Schedule and receive notifications for upcoming church events. Review past programs and share in the vibrancy of the church calendar.
  • Consolidated Christian literature: upto 300 different versions of the Bible, hymns and more

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: Flutter for cross-platform compatibility.
  • Backend: Node.js and Express for RESTful APIs.
  • Database: MongoDB for scalable data, SQLite for offline access.
  • Real-Time: Socket.IO for notifications.

Implementation

  • Developed key modules:
    • Repository Module: Centralized sermons, hymns, and events.
    • Event Management Module: Event scheduling and notifications.
    • Literature:Consolidated Christian literature Bibles & hymns.
  • Enhanced security: bcryptjs for passwords, JWT for authentication, and Helmet for protection.
